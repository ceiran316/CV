4th Year Project

Meetings Bot for Watson Work Space

This project is a based around a bot which deals and organises meetings.

The user will have the ability to create, update and delete meetings.

When each of the functionality has been utilised the invities will receive and email or an sms notification informing them that a meeting has been created and they are invited, the details of a meeting they have been invited to has changed or a meeting the have been invited to has been cancelled.

Technologies used: JavaScript, NodeJS & Domino 10 API’s, slack API, Glitch

2nd Year Project

GAA Score Centre

This was a college group project designed to record and store statistics and figures from the 2017 GAA National Football League for customers to view and analyse.

Feedback from surveys gave us a clear direction to what our potential users expected and demanded.

Our object-oriented design was fundamental in planning the flow and organisation of our project and gave us a great foundation to start from.

Features included admin and member login, admin possesses create, update and delete functionality, users can search team sheets/ line-ups, view games and league statistics.

Technologies used: Visio, Oracle SQL, Java, JavaScript, Java Play Framework, Scala
